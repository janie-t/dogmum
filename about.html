About Us

Friendly, personal care

Hi, I'm Janie - The Dog Mum. I provide doggy daycare while you work or overnight stays. We are in Avalon, Lower Hutt.

I love dogs, and they have always been a part of my family. My day job is as a software developer. I work from home some days, and most of the week am at Thankyou Payroll in Wellington. They are a dog friendly workplace and welcome furry friends.

Your furbaby will come and stay in our house, and have one-on-one care and attention. I only look after one client at a time, up to two dogs. If you have more than two, just ring me for a chat first.

They will be hanging out with me during the day, and usually have at least a few dog friends to socialise with (only if they want to!). We will get out for at least one decent walk - more if they stay overnight.

Our property is fully fenced and secure, with a large backyard to play in. We love going to Petone Dog Beach, perfect for walks, swims and doggy socialising. I have two school-aged children who are experienced and careful with dogs.
